# ViralClip Swarm Pitch For Influencers

## One-Line Positioning

ViralClip Swarm turns one long video into short clips, vertical crops, captions, metadata, and preview assets without a manual timeline workflow.

## Why A Creator Should Care

- faster first pass on long content
- vertical crop that can track faces
- animated captions built into the pipeline
- platform-specific caption suggestions
- preview thumbnails and collage assets for review

## What You Can Show

## What Makes It Different

- local-first workflow instead of only cloud SaaS
- scriptable and reproducible
- benchmark and evidence output, not just final videos
- built for editors and creators who want control
