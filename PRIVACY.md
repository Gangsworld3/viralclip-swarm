# Privacy Policy (Project-Level)

## What This Tool Processes

ViralClip Swarm processes media inputs (video/audio), transcripts, and generated clip metadata.

## Data Flow

- Local mode: data stays on the local machine by default.
- Cloud AI mode: transcript/media-derived content may be sent to configured third-party providers.

## What Is Not Collected by Default

The project does not include built-in user analytics, ad tracking, or telemetry pipelines by default.

## User Responsibilities

Operators are responsible for:

- obtaining lawful rights to process uploaded media
- informing end users when third-party AI providers are used
- configuring retention and deletion behavior for generated outputs and logs

## Sensitive Data Guidance

Do not process highly sensitive personal data through third-party AI providers unless legal/compliance requirements are satisfied.
